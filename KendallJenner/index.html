BIOGRAPHY

Kendall Nicole Jenner (born November 3, 1995) is an American model, media personality, socialite and businesswoman. She is a daughter of Kris Jenner and Caitlyn Jenner, and rose to fame in the reality television show Keeping Up with the Kardashians, in which she starred for 20 seasons and nearly 15 years from 2007 to 2021. The success of the show led to the creation of multiple spin-off series including Kourtney and Khloe Take Miami (2009), Kourtney and Kim Take New York (2011), Khloe & Lamar (2011), Rob & Chyna (2016) and Life of Kylie (2017). Following the decision to end their reality show, in 2022 she and her family starred in the reality television series The Kardashians on Hulu.
Jenner began modeling at the age of 14. After working in commercial print ad campaigns and photoshoots, she had breakout seasons in 2014 and 2015, walking the runways for high-fashion designers during the New York, Milan, and Paris fashion weeks. Jenner has appeared in campaigns, editorials, and cover shoots for LOVE and various international Vogue editions, and is a brand ambassador for Estée Lauder. Jenner made her debut at No. 16 on Forbes magazine's 2015 list of top-earning models, with an estimated annual income of US$4 million. In 2017, Jenner was named the world's highest-paid model by Forbes, ousting model Gisele Bündchen who had been leading the list since 2002. In 2021, she launched the tequila brand 818 Tequila.

CAREER

TELEVISION CAREER: In 2007, Kris Jenner pitched a reality TV show idea to Ryan Seacrest, resulting in the creation of "Keeping Up with the Kardashians" (KUWTK), debuting in 2007 on E! network. The show, featuring Jenner's family, became a massive success, leading to spin-offs like "Kourtney and Kim Take Miami" and "Kourtney & Khloé Take the Hamptons." After almost 15 years, KUWTK ended in 2021. In 2022, the family returned with a new show, "The Kardashians," on Hulu.
MODELING CAREER: Jenner started modeling at 13, signed by Wilhelmina Models in 2009. Her early gigs included a Forever 21 campaign. By 2014, she transitioned to high fashion, working with renowned designers and signing with The Society Management. Jenner's success continued, walking for major brands and becoming an official Estée Lauder representative in 2014.
BUSINESS CAREER: Jenner ventured into various business endeavors. She co-created nail lacquers, became a Creative Director for Gillette, and partnered with Glamhouse for a jewelry collection. In 2015, she launched Kendall + Kylie with PacSun, followed by collaborations with Topshop. The sisters also created mobile apps and launched a Kendall and Kylie app. Their fashion brand faced controversy in 2017 over vintage T-shirts, leading to public apologies.
Jenner further extended her business ventures to oral care, collaborating with Moon, and entered the skincare industry with Proactiv. In 2020, she launched Kendall Jenner x Kylie Cosmetics. She continued diversifying with a limited-edition toothpaste collaboration with designer Heron Preston on StockX. In 2021, Jenner introduced her tequila brand, "818 Tequila," which officially launched in May 2021.
